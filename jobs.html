Work at JavaJam

Barista

As a barista, you will represent our coffee business to the public and be in charge of how they are treated. While making sure that our clients have a pleasurable and fulfilling experience, you'll make and serve a choice of hot and cold beverages.
Hourly Wage: $15.00

  • Prepare and serve tea and coffee beverages.
  • Maintain a spotless workspace.
  • Observe the rules of health and safety.
  • Interact professionally and frequently with consumers.

Shift Supervisor

You will manage the coffee shop's operations while working as a shift supervisor. You'll make sure that our service operates effectively and efficiently while upholding the highest standards of quality.
Hourly Wage: $18.00

  • During the shift, manage and instruct the baristas
  • Encourage superior service to ensure client happiness.
  • In order to satisfy them, you must address consumer complaints.
  • Keep an eye on the supply and place orders as necessary.
